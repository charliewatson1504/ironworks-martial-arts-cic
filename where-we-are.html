where we are

Ironworks Martial Arts CIC is only 5 minutes from the Junction 62 of the A1 with access to the A690. We are approximately 2 miles from Durham City Centre to the west, 8 miles from Sunderland to the east and 14 miles from Newcastle to the north. We have access to numerous bus links and a walking route into Durham City. We also benefit from free on-site parking. We are a short 10-minute drive from Durham Train Station, a 5-minute drive from Dragonville Retail Park with hotel accommodation within walking distance. Please contact us should you require further information.
